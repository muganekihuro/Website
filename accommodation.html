ACCOMMODATION

Value lodge hotel

Value lodge hotel is a city accommodation that aims to provide a comfortable and affordable stay for travelers. Nested around vancouver island this hotel offers a range of amenities and services to ensure a pleasant experience for guests.

Ocean Crest hotel

Indulge your taste buds at the hotel's onsite restaurant, where a culinary journey awaits. The chefs at Small Crest Hotel pride themselves on crafting delectable dishes that showcase the region's fresh and local ingredients. Enjoy your meal in a relaxed setting with views of the surrounding natural beauty.

Riding food hotel

Riding Food Hotel offers a range of thoughtfully designed accommodations that blend modern amenities with a cozy ambiance. From well-appointed rooms to spacious suites, each space is crafted to ensure a comfortable and relaxing stay.

seascape inn

Wake up to the soothing sound of waves and panoramic views of the ocean. Seascape Inn's prime location ensures that guests can enjoy breathtaking sunsets and the calming rhythm of the sea from the comfort of their rooms or from designated scenic spots within the property.

Reefpoint cottage

Reefpoint Cottage offers a unique and intimate lodging experience. The cottage is thoughtfully designed to blend modern amenities with a touch of rustic charm, providing guests with a comfortable and welcoming space to unwind.

Beach acres resort

Beach Acres Resort features a range of accommodations, from cozy cabins to spacious beachfront suites. Each lodging option is designed to provide a comfortable retreat, with amenities that cater to both families and couples looking for a peaceful getaway.

West View Marina

West View Marina is a picturesque and inviting destination nestled in the heart of the Vancouver Island region. Boasting stunning waterfront views and a tranquil atmosphere, this marina is a haven for boating enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. With a range of amenities and services, West View Marina provides a perfect setting for memorable experiences on the water. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat or an exciting adventure, West View Marina offers the ideal blend of serenity and recreational opportunities for visitors to immerse themselves in the beauty of the surrounding landscape

Two Eagles Lodge

Two Eagles Lodge on Vancouver Island is a cozy retreat, seamlessly blending rustic charm with modern comfort. Surrounded by natural beauty, the lodge offers warm hospitality, comfortable accommodations, and a peaceful atmosphere. Whether exploring the outdoors or relaxing on the lodge grounds, Two Eagles Lodge provides a rejuvenating escape for those seeking tranquility on Vancouver Island.

Sugar Bear Cove

Sugar Bear Cove on Vancouver Island is a secluded paradise, perfect for a tranquil escape surrounded by nature's beauty. With pristine beaches and lush greenery, it offers a serene backdrop for relaxation and exploration, making it an ideal retreat for those seeking a picturesque coastal sanctuary.
