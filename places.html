PLACES TO VISIT

North island

Beginning just an hour north of Campbell River and stretching towards Sayward, the North Island region unfolds to the windswept tip at Cape Scott, encompassing the rugged Pacific coast and extending east to the whale-rich waters, including sections of the Great Bear Rainforest and BC mainland. Top of the wishlist for visitors are whale and bear sightings, and the region offers group outings on comfortable cabin cruisers or thrilling open-air Zodiacs in pursuit of orcas, humpback whales, porpoises, Steller sea lions, Minke whales, and harbour seals. The prime season from May to October is ideal for nautical excursions from Northern Vancouver Island to the mainland's grizzly bear habitats.

cowichan

In every Cowichan community, a lively arts scene thrives, featuring nearly two dozen arts groups and several internationally acclaimed artists. Immerse yourself in musical and theatrical performances that showcase the region's creative spirit. Explore home artist studios and galleries to discover the craftsmanship of local sculptors, woodworkers, potters, painters, and glass blowers, each contributing to Cowichan's vibrant and diverse artistic landscape.

Pacific Rim

International travelers and those seeking an escape from urban life in Vancouver, Victoria, and Seattle can find a diverse array of experiences here. From exploring inland trails, lakes, and mountain bike routes to discovering renowned waterways teeming with salmon, unraveling the ancient mysteries of the coastal rainforest, and embracing the ever-changing beauty of the open Pacific, this destination has it all. Starting from the eastern coastline of Central Island, the journey takes you to Port Alberni, a historic deepwater inland port now flourishing with a growing eco-tourism industry, rooted in its past as a prominent salmon fishing hub.

The Southern Gulf Islands

The Southern Gulf Islands off the coast of British Columbia, offer a tranquil escape with their natural beauty and artistic communities. From lush landscapes to artisan markets, these islands provide a perfect blend of outdoor adventure and cultural exploration, making them an ideal retreat for those seeking a memorable getaway in the Pacific Northwest.

Sunshine Coast

The Sunshine Coast in British Columbia is a coastal haven known for its stunning landscapes and vibrant communities. With picturesque beaches, lush forests, and charming seaside villages, it's a perfect destination for those seeking a laid-back coastal lifestyle. From outdoor adventures to exploring local art, the Sunshine Coast offers a diverse range of experiences, making it an ideal escape for relaxation or adventure.
