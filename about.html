ABOUT VANCOUVER ISLAND

Nestled on Canada's West Coast, Vancouver Island spans 460 kilometers from Victoria to Cape Scott Provincial Park. This idyllic island is a tapestry of ancient rainforests, rugged coastlines, and the Pacific's cool embrace. A haven for outdoor enthusiasts, it offers kayaking, lush trails, and serene beaches. Beyond nature's allure, Vancouver Island's vibrant communities contribute to its cultural richness and laid-back lifestyle, creating a harmonious blend of coastal living and natural splendor.
